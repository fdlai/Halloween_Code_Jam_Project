Our Special Events

  • Haunted House

    Experience the ultimate thrill at our Haunted House, where fear comes to life! Our expertly designed, chilling corridors are filled with lurking horrors and nightmarish surprises at every turn. From ghastly ghosts to malevolent monsters, our haunted house promises an unforgettable journey into the macabre. Get ready for a heart-pounding experience that will haunt your dreams for years to come.

  • Escape Room

    Brace yourself for the ultimate test of your wits and courage at our Horror Escape Room experience! Step into a world of spine-tingling suspense and heart-pounding thrills, where you and your team will be trapped in a nightmarish scenario. Solve intricate puzzles, decipher cryptic clues, and unlock the mysteries that will set you free. Gather your bravest friends and prepare for an unforgettable adventure.

  • Carnival Games

    Get ready to immerse yourself in a world of eerie fun and excitement. Our carnival is brimming with devilish delights, from haunted ring tosses to pumpkin bowling, and everything in between. Challenge your friends and family to test their skills and bravery as you navigate through a maze of hair-raising games and thrilling prizes. With a cackling ringmaster, creepy clowns, and ghoulish attractions, you're in for a hauntingly good time.

  • Concerts

    Every Friday & Saturday Night in October, join us for a hauntingly good time at our Halloween Concert. As the moon rises and the spirits awaken, our stage will come alive with electrifying performances that will send shivers down your spine. From ghoulish rock anthems to eerie symphonic melodies, our lineup promises to keep you mesmerized all night long. Get your tickets now and let the music of the night cast its spell on you.
